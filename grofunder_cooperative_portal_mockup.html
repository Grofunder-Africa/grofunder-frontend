Declare your clusters

Clusters are your own groupings of farmers. Declare them once — every farmer you add is then assigned from this list, so records stay clean and consistent.

Appoint each cluster head from its own members — the dropdowns fill as you register farmers in the next step. Growth Circles are separate: farmers form those themselves in the app.
